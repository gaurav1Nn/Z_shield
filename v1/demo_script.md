# ZShield Hackathon Demo Script

This script guides you through recording a flawless demo video of the ZShield platform.

## üìã Preparation
1.  **Browser:** Use Chrome or Edge in **Full Screen** (F11).
2.  **Zoom:** Set browser zoom to **100%** or **110%** for better visibility.
3.  **Clean State:** Refresh the page (`Ctrl+R`) before starting recording.

## üé¨ Scene 1: The Hook (Home Page)
**Action:**
1.  Start recording.
2.  **Hover** over the "Private Swaps." text to show the glow effect.
3.  **Hover** over the "Zero Exposure." text to show the shimmer animation.
4.  **Scroll down slowly** to trigger the **Parallax Effect** on the text (it moves slower than the background).
5.  Continue scrolling to reveal the **"How It Works"** section.
6.  **Hover** over each of the 3 cards (Connect Wallet, Swap Privately, Receive Assets) to show the hover effects.

**Narration (Optional):**
"Welcome to ZShield, the next-generation privacy-first DEX. Our platform ensures zero exposure for your trades using advanced shielded pools."

## üé¨ Scene 2: Connecting (The "Wow" Factor)
**Action:**
1.  Scroll back up to the top.
2.  Click the **"Connect Wallet"** button (top right or center).
3.  The **Wallet Modal** appears with a glassmorphism effect.
4.  Select **"Zcash Wallet"** (or any option).
5.  **Wait** for the "Connecting..." animation.
6.  See the success state and the wallet address appear in the navbar (`zs1q...7k3m`).

**Narration:**
"Connecting is seamless. We support unified Zcash addresses and major privacy wallets."

## üé¨ Scene 3: The Swap (Core Feature)
**Action:**
1.  Go to the **Swap Card** (center of hero section).
2.  **Select Tokens:**
    *   From: **ZEC** (Zcash)
    *   To: **ETH** (Ethereum)
3.  **Enter Amount:** Type `10` in the input field.
    *   *Notice the "Exchange Rate" and "Fully Shielded Transaction" badge appear.*
4.  **Hover** over the "Fully Shielded Transaction" badge to show the privacy checks (Hidden Sender, Receiver, Amount).
5.  Click the **"Swap Privately"** button.
6.  Watch the **Liquid Button** animation turn into a "Processing..." state.
7.  Wait 3 seconds for it to complete (it resets automatically).

**Narration:**
"Swapping is intuitive. ZShield automatically routes your trade through a shielded pool, breaking the on-chain link between sender and receiver."

## üé¨ Scene 4: The Ecosystem (Other Pages)
**Action:**
1.  Click **"Pool"** in the navbar.
    *   Show the "Add Liquidity" card and "Your Positions".
    *   Hover over the liquidity positions to show the glow.
2.  Click **"Orders"** in the navbar.
    *   Click between "Open Orders" and "Order History" tabs to show the smooth underline animation.
3.  Click **"Analytics"** in the navbar.
    *   **Hover** over the **Volume Chart** bars to show the tooltips.
    *   Scroll down to "Privacy Metrics" and "Recent Activity".
    *   Point out the "100% Shielded" badge.

## üé¨ Scene 5: Conclusion
**Action:**
1.  Click the **ZShield Logo** to return to the home page.
2.  Do one final slow scroll or mouse movement over the 3D background.
3.  Stop recording.
